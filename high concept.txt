steer your spaceship to avoid the enemies fire and shoot them back to save the earth from destruction! 
progressively harder levels and enemies keep you on your toes at all times.


features

simple controls, deep gameplay
many different enemies and levels
simple pixel graphics
different powerups and weapons


platforms: android, iOs (pc)


more details

levels progress without interruption; more of a wave system, but can be resumed from saves

weapons: machine gun thing, slower rpg with a blast radius
	rpg ammo is dropped from enemies, when out return to smg

controls: left third to go left, same for right, bottom middle to shoot smg, top for rpg if has ammo, also top third above left and right shoot smg

more advanced enemies can dodge bullets, some like staying in groups, some dont, some are good at aiming
